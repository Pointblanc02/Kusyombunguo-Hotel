Our Room

We have a wide variety of rooms for you to choose from

Triple Bed

A Triple Bed Hotel Room is designed to accommodate three guests comfortably. It typically features three separate beds or a combination of one double/queen bed and one single bed, depending on the room layout.

Standard Room

A basic room with standard amenities like a bed, desk, television, and bathroom.

Deluxe Room

Larger and more comfortable than a standard room, often with upgraded furnishings and amenities

Suite

A spacious room with separate living and sleeping areas, often featuring luxurious amenities.

Studio Room

A room with an open-plan layout, combining sleeping, dining, and living areas.

Cabana

A room near the pool or beach, sometimes with direct access to these areas.
